Hey there! I'm a software engineer who enjoys a good debugging challenge. I specialize in adapting and learning new technologies quickly to make an awesome product.

I graduated from Dartmouth College with my bachelor's degree in Environmental Studies and Native American Studies. I started my career as a paralegal, hopeful that I would eventually work in advocating in environmental issues and Indigenous Rights. Eventually I moved into litigation support technologies where I focused on database management and data conversion. I yearned for a career where I could grow my skillset and challenge myself with more complex problem solving, thus I ventured into software development.

I've spent the past year diving into programming. I graduated from the Grace Hopper Program at Fullstack Academy. I'm currently serving as a fellow for the program where I've had the opportunity to mentor current students. I'm looking for an position where I can continue to develop my skillset and contibute to projects which make a positive impact on our world.

In my spare time, I play roller derby with Gotham Girls Roller Derby. I love coaching and am passionate about mentoring the next generation of great skaters. I am most proud of skating with the inaugural Team Indigenous at the 2018 Roller Derby World Cup.
